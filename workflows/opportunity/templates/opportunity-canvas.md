# Opportunity Canvas — Schema

> A collaborative working surface for the **convergent close of diamond one**: aligning the team on the problem worth solving. Fill it on a whiteboard (Miro / FigJam). Frameworks below are **optional lenses** — use whatever unlocks discussion. Time-box it; a gap with a noted risk is a valid outcome.

---

## 1. User & customer cohorts
> *Who has this problem? List the distinct cohorts. Note who is a user vs. an economic buyer where they differ.*

_(empty — for the team)_

## 2. Customer problem
> *What problem do we believe these cohorts have? State it plainly — framework-agnostic.*

_(empty — for the team)_

## 3. Solutions today (competitor / alternatives)
> *How do these cohorts solve this problem now? Direct competitors, indirect workarounds, and "do nothing."*

_(empty — for the team)_

## 4. Why the business should care
> *What challenges does this create for the business, and what's the potential impact of solving it?*
> *Optional: a rough value/time calculation (e.g. hours saved × volume, or revenue at risk).*

_(empty — for the team) · value/time calc: optional_

## 5. Pain/gain + JTBD / empathy pass
> *Deepen the problem. Optional lenses — pick what fits:*
> - **Pains / gains:** what frustrates them today; what would delight them.
> - **JTBD:** "When ___, I want to ___, so I can ___."
> - **Empathy map:** says / thinks / does / feels.

_(empty — for the team) · choose the lens that unlocks discussion_

## 6. Adoption strategy (per cohort)
> *For each cohort: how would they discover, try, and adopt a solution? What would have to be true?*

_(empty — for the team)_

## 7. Impact stack-rank (tees up ideation)
> *Rank the problems/sub-problems by impact. This is the hand-off into ideation — keep it simple (e.g. High / Med / Low, or a quick 1–n ordering).*

_(empty — for the team)_

---

> 🔚 **The canvas ends here.** Ideation — generating and shaping solutions — happens **off-canvas**, in the Ideation Workflow. This canvas exists to align on the problem and tee up that ideation, nothing more.
